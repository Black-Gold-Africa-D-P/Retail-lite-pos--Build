Frequently Asked Questions Maswali Yanayoulizwa Mara kwa Mara

Find answers to common questions about Retail-Lite POS

What devices can run Retail-Lite POS?

Retail-Lite POS runs on any PC or Android smartphone or tablet with Android 5.0 or higher. We recommend devices with at least 2GB of RAM for optimal performance. An iOS version is currently in development Use Web Version.

How does the offline mode work?

When you're offline, the app stores all transaction data locally on your device. Once you connect to the internet, it automatically syncs with our secure cloud servers. You can continue selling without interruption even during network outages.

Is M-Pesa integration included in all plans?

Yes, all subscription plans include M-Pesa integration at no extra cost. This allows you to receive payments directly through the app and automatically reconcile them with your sales records.

How much does Retail-Lite POS cost?

We offer flexible pricing plans starting from just KSh 999 per month. Early waitlist members will receive a special discount and 3 months free when we launch. Full pricing details will be announced closer to launch date.

Can I connect a receipt printer?

Yes, Retail-Lite POS supports most Bluetooth thermal printers. We also offer digital receipts that can be sent via SMS or WhatsApp to your customers, saving paper and reducing costs.

When will Retail-Lite POS launch?

We're currently in the final testing phase and plan to launch within the next 2-3 months. Join our waitlist to be notified as soon as we launch and to secure your early access benefits.
